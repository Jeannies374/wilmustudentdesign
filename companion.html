Companion Dogs


Companion dogs are those best friends we share our daily lives with and that we look to for comfort.

IHeartDogs.com lists the 10 best breeds for companion dogs as: Cavalier King Charles Spaniel, Basset Hound, Italian Greyhound, Bullmastiff, Pekingnese, Yorkshire Terrier, Lhasa Apso, Greyhound, Bulldog, and ShihTzu.

The dog above is Jasmine and she is a devoted companion dog. National Geographic tells the story of how the rescue dog helped care for other animals including an orphaned roe deer named Bramble.
