Working Dogs

Working dogs are of many different breeds and sizes. According to Cesar Milan's website the top ten breeds of working dogs are: Boxer, Great Pyrenees, Doberman and German Pinscher, Rottweiler, Husky. Akita, Bloodhound, Golden Retriever, Labrador Retriever, and German Shepherd.

Working dogs perform many important jobs to help us all. One of these jobs is to assist police officers in protecting us. The dog in the photo above is one of those special dogs. This dog is named Slim and he was featured on PetCentral on Chewy.com.

Slim's partner is quoted in the article as stating, "Slim stayed the course and found the bad guy. His success can be chalked up to police training, and the canine's powerful sense of smell. Even after years of seeing Slim in action, Corcoran is still in awe of his powerful tracking abilities."
